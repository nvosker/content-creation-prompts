# TikTok Script Generator

## Description
Write short-form video scripts (15-60 seconds) that hook viewers in the first 1-2 seconds and deliver value or entertainment that keeps people watching until the end.

## Prompt Text
```
You are a TikTok content strategist who writes scripts that stop the scroll and get people to watch until the end.

I want to create a TikTok about: [TOPIC/IDEA - e.g., "3 copywriting tricks that boost conversions"]
Video length: [15 / 30 / 60 SECONDS]
Hook type: [HOOK STYLE - e.g., "contrarian take," "surprising tip," "satisfying transformation"]
My niche: [YOUR NICHE - e.g., "copywriting and marketing"]
Audience: [WHO YOU'RE TALKING TO - e.g., "solopreneurs and content creators"]

Write a TikTok script that:
1. HOOK (0-2 seconds): Opens with a question, surprising statement, or visual transformation that makes people stop scrolling
2. SETUP (2-5 seconds): Briefly explain the problem or context (keep it SHORT)
3. VALUE/ENTERTAINMENT (5-15 seconds): Deliver the tip, trick, or payoff
   - If teaching: explain 1-3 concepts clearly
   - If entertainment: build to a satisfying punchline or moment
4. CALL-TO-ACTION (last 2 seconds): Tell viewers what to do next (save, comment, follow)

For each section, include:
- Exact spoken words (what you say on camera)
- Visual cue (what's happening in the video)
- Pacing (roughly how many seconds each section takes)

Rules for TikTok scripts:
- First 0.5 seconds must grab attention (question, text overlay, B-roll, surprise)
- Avoid jargon; speak like you're texting a friend
- Include text overlays (captions help with watch time and sound-off viewing)
- End frames matter—the final 1 second should feel satisfying or intriguing
- Keep energy high; pacing should match the audio (don't let it drag)

Format as a scene-by-scene breakdown with timestamps.
```

## Example Input

**Video Length:** 30 seconds  
**Hook Type:** Contrarian take (most people do it wrong)  
**Niche:** Copywriting/content marketing  
**Audience:** Content creators and solopreneurs, ages 20-40

## Pro Tips

**Tip 1: The First 0.5 Seconds Are Everything**  
TikTok's algorithm measures if people swipe away immediately. A jarring question, text overlay, or B-roll change in the first half-second massively improves your watch-through rate. Invest in a killer hook.

**Tip 2: Text Overlays Increase Watch Time by 40%+**  
TikTok viewers often watch without sound (muted at work, etc.). Add captions for everything important. This also helps people follow complex ideas in short formats.

**Tip 3: End With a Satisfying Frame**  
The last frame people see (and pause on if they save) should feel complete. Either a satisfying visual transformation, a clear takeaway text overlay, or you looking directly at the camera with a knowing smile. Don't let it fizzle.
