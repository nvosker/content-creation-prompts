# Blog-to-Social Content Converter

## Description
Transform a single blog post into 15-20 unique social media posts across multiple platforms (LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram). Includes platform-specific formatting and hook strategies.

## Prompt Text
```
You are an expert content repurposing strategist who turns one blog post into endless social content.

I have a blog post about: [TOPIC]
Blog post key insight: [MAIN TAKEAWAY - e.g., "most cold emails fail because they don't show empathy first"]
Blog post length: [ROUGH LENGTH - e.g., "2,500 words"]
Target audience: [WHO YOU'RE TALKING TO - e.g., "sales professionals and entrepreneurs"]
Platforms to target: [e.g., "LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram, TikTok"]

Generate social posts by extracting ideas from the blog and reformatting for each platform:

**For LinkedIn (3-4 posts):**
- 1 authority-building post (quote or insight from the blog with commentary)
- 1 poll-style post (engagement-driving)
- 1 story-style post (vulnerable share or case study)

**For Twitter/X (4-5 posts):**
- 1 thread (8-10 tweets based on main framework)
- 1-2 hot take posts (contrarian angles)
- 2-3 quick tips (shareable, retweet-worthy)

**For Instagram (3-4 posts):**
- 1 carousel post script (teaching framework visually)
- 1 caption-heavy post (vulnerable story)
- 1-2 quote graphics (with caption to drive saves)

**For TikTok (2-3 scripts):**
- 1-2 15-30 second hook + tip videos
- 1 talking-head video (you sharing the core insight)

**For Email/Newsletter (2-3 variations):**
- 1 email highlighting the blog with unique insight
- 1 email from the blog's data/stats
- 1 email addressing objections the blog raises

For each post, include:
- Platform-specific formatting
- Hook (what makes them stop scrolling)
- Main idea (what you're teaching/sharing)
- CTA (what you want them to do)
- Hashtags (if relevant)
- Character count (if applicable)

Avoid:
- Direct copy-paste of blog text (reframe for platform)
- Over-hashtag spam (limit to 3-5 max per platform)
- Generic CTAs ("Share this post!")
- Ignoring platform norms (TikTok ≠ LinkedIn tone)

Format as a repurposing brief with all posts ready to schedule.
```

## Pro Tips

**Tip 1: Don't Just Copy-Paste Blog Text**  
Reframe the core insights for each platform's tone and format. A Twitter thread isn't a shortened blog post—it's the same idea told as a series of connected thoughts with breaks and surprises.

**Tip 2: Schedule the Repurposing**  
Spread posts over 3-4 weeks instead of dumping them all at once. This gives each platform time to perform and prevents audience fatigue.

**Tip 3: Use Unique Angles for Each Platform**  
LinkedIn: Authority. Twitter: Contrarian takes and tips. Instagram: Visual and storytelling. TikTok: Entertainment and hooks. Same message, different angles.
